The Clock Emporium

The Clock Emporium is a large retail shop situated at the St David’s Shopping centre Cardiff selling new and second hand clocks. The business was established in 1920 by the current owner’s great great grandfather who started the business repairing clocks in his garden shed almost 100 years ago. Now, in our 100th years anniversary we have launched a website and great discounts as well. Celebrate with us! Buy a new, shiny clock!
